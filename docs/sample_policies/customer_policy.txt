# InsightFlow Enterprise Customer Policy Manual

## Section 1: Refund Policy

### 1.1 Enterprise Client Refunds
Enterprise clients (contract value above $5,000/year) are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of subscription start. After 30 days, a prorated refund is issued for unused months.

### 1.2 Mid-Market Refunds
Mid-Market clients are eligible for a full refund within 14 days. After 14 days, no refund is issued but a credit is applied to the next billing cycle.

### 1.3 SMB Refunds
SMB clients receive a 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.

---

## Section 2: SLA Commitments

### 2.1 Uptime Guarantee
InsightFlow AI guarantees 99.9% uptime for all Enterprise clients. Downtime exceeding this threshold results in a service credit of 10% of the monthly fee per hour of excess downtime.

### 2.2 Support Response Times
- Critical (P1): 1-hour response, 4-hour resolution
- High (P2): 4-hour response, 24-hour resolution  
- Medium (P3): 1 business day response
- Low (P4): 3 business day response

---

## Section 3: Data Retention

All customer data is retained for 7 years in compliance with standard accounting practices. Customers may request data deletion subject to legal hold requirements.

---

## Section 4: Usage Limits

Enterprise plans include unlimited API calls. Mid-Market plans are capped at 100,000 API calls/month. SMB plans are capped at 10,000 API calls/month. Overages are billed at $0.01 per 1,000 calls.
